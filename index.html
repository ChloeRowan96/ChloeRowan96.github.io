Clonmacnoise

Early Christian Monastic Site

Siobhán McCarthy

10/07/17

A wonderful and peaceful place!

This 6th century monastic site is in great condition. It was amazing to see a round tower and such an array of churches. Our tour guide was very interesting and gave us lots of information not only about the site but regarding the surrounding area. We took the Viking ship from Athlone which really enhanced the experience. I would ring ahead to find out the times of the public tours, don't miss out!
